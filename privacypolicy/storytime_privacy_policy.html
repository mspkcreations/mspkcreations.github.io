Our Commitment

Story Time is designed for children and families. We comply with the Children's Online Privacy Protection Act (COPPA) and prioritize children's privacy.

Children's Privacy: This app is directed to children under 13. We do not collect personal information from children without parental consent.

Data Collection

We do not collect, store, or process any personal data ourselves. Story Time does not have servers and operates entirely through third-party services. All data handling is managed by Google.

Third-Party Data Handling

  • Google Drive: Stores and syncs your stories across devices
  • Google AdMob: Collects device identifiers for serving child-safe, COPPA-compliant ads
